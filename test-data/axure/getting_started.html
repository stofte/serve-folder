Share with Axure Cloud

You can publish your projects to Axure Cloud by clicking the Share button at the top-right. You'll get a link to the published prototype that you can share with others to view on any device with a web browser. Try it out!


Once published, you and your team can comment directly on prototypes, inspect them for CSS and layout, and receive Slack, Microsoft Teams and email notifications when changes are made to the project. You can also share directly in JIRA and Confluence with an Axure integration (axure.com/integrations).
